A company trying to let people enjoy fresh filtered water without having to pay premium prices OEM charge. We want people to enjoy premium tasting water without paying the premium price. Companies like Samsung, LG, and Kenmore charge a minimum of ~$30 for replacement water filters.

NSF stands for the National Sanitation Foundation. In short, these certifications are the leading industry standards for filtration products and systems.

NSF 42 certification establishes minimum requirements for systems desinged to reduce non-health-related contaminants.

NSF 53 certification establishes minimum requirements for systems desinged to reduce specified health-related contaminants. This standard offers over 50 contaminant reduction claims. Some of the most popular include lead, cryptosporidium, VOCs, and chromium

The average life span of a filter is 3-6 months, or can treat about 300 gallons of water.
